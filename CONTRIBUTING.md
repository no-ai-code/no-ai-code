# How to Contribute Your Dev Story

Welcome to the “Developer Extinction Log” of NO AI CODE!  
Share your pure hand-coding journey, skill decay feelings, and your personal “extinction countdown” estimate.

### How to Participate (Easiest Way: Open an Issue)
1. Click **Issues** → **New issue** at the top of the repo
2. Use any title (suggested: `[Dev Story] Your Name/Handle - Years of Experience`)
3. Copy-paste the template below into the issue body, fill it out, and submit
